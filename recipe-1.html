Creamy Garlic Pasta

← Back to recipes

Ingredients

  • 200g pasta
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Cook pasta according to package instructions.
  2. Sauté garlic in olive oil until golden.
  3. Add cream, salt, pepper, and simmer.
  4. Mix pasta and sauce together.
